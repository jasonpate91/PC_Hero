Power Supply


What is a Power Supply?

A Power Supply provides power to all the components that require power within the computer.


Why is a Power Supply important?

Without power, none of the components will function and the computer will never turn on! Additionally, it's important to ensure that the power supply generates enough power to cover the required wattage to run everything. It's not uncommon for someone to replace a component only to discover they need more power to run the new component!
